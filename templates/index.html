NoVA-SCAN

What is NoVA-SCAN

NoVA-Scan is an AI-powered web app that automatically detects key areas from your image — the Eyes, Palm, and Nail Beds — using advanced object detection technology (YOLO).

Eyes

Let NoVA-Scan detect your eye region automatically for a clear and accurate image.

Palm

Your palm area is identified instantly, helping AI prepare it for detailed analysis.

Nail Beds

NoVA-Scan pinpoints your nail area for focused, accurate detection.

How It Works

1
Capture or Upload

Upload an image showing your eyes, palm, or nails using your camera or device.

2
AI Detection

NoVA-Scan uses YOLO AI to automatically locate and outline the key regions.

3
Review Results

View the detected areas and download the processed results for analysis.
